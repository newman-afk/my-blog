`<Fragment>`, often used via `<>...</>` syntax, lets you group elements without a wrapper node.

```jsx
<>
  <OneChild />
  <AnotherChild />
</>
```

[官方文档链接](https://react.dev/reference/react/Fragment)

唯一要点：If you want to pass `key` to a Fragment, you can’t use the `<>...</>` syntax. You have to explicitly `import Fragment from 'react'` and render `<Fragment key={yourKey}>...</Fragment>`.
